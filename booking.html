Regestration

Registration to climb unsupervised at Irish Climbing Experience is mandatory on your first visit. Each new member wishing to climb unsupervised must deminstrate to an ICE staff member basic climbing safety skills. This will include tying onto a rope and belaying with a belaying device. The €5 Registration is an Annual charge
