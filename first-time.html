What to Expect

Arrival

  • First things First, mask up. We take safety seriously on and off the wall.
  • Check in with a staff member at the Front Desk.
  • They will explain our waiver form to you and answer any questions that you might have.
  • They will show you to a locker where you can keep your things while you climb.
  • If you are rope climbing we will rent you a harness, shoes and chalk bag.
  • If you are bouldering we will rent you shoes and a chalk bag.

Introduction

  • One of our instructors will take you over how to put on your harness.
  • You will be given an introductory lesson on how to clip into the route, how to belay, safety check your partner and how to climb.
  • All our routes are colour coded which you instructor will explain to you.
  • If you are bouldering then we will show you how to safely climb and come off the wall.
  • We will answer any questions that you have and will make sure you feel safe and we feel safe to let you climb.

Time to Climb

  • Find a circuit by using the colour-coordinated grading scale.
  • If you send it, move on to the next one!
  • Keep an eye out for other climbers who might be around you.
  • Enjoy Yourself!!!
