Bouldering

Bouldering is rock climbing stripped down to its raw essentials. Leaving behind ropes and harnesses and just using climbing shoes and a bag of chalk over safety mats. ICEs bouldering area is one of the best in the North West of Ireland. We have over 800m of bouldering problems which include areas that emulate caves and challenging overhangs. Our bouldering room is constantly being upgraded with new challenges by our experienced inhouse route setters.

Top Roping

The idea behind top-roping is that there is already an anchor attached at the top of the route. One end of the rope is attached to the belayer, drawn up through the top anchor, and the other end is attached to the climber. We have recently expanded our top rope area to include about 75 rope routes. Our top rope walls which are up to 12 meters include overhang and slab features with routes for every level and type of climber.

Lead Climbing

Lead climbing you take the rope up with you as you climb, clipping it in to the wall as you go. You may not think that it is much more physically demanding than top-rope, but it is. Our lead wall has over 40 different routes with a mixture of styles. From dynamic and crack to slab and over hang routes, we have something for everyone.

Fitness Facilities

Our strength training and fitness area is specifically geared toward climbers and includes hangboards, pull up bars, rings, rowing machines and more. Our fitness facilities are free to use for all members of ICE.

Viewing Area

Looking to relax after a hard session? Or maybe you are here to just watch. We have designed a viewing gallery where you can relax with a tea or coffee and have a birds eye view of all the climbers in action.
