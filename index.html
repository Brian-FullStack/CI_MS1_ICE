About Us

Irish Climbing Experience has climbing for all ages and abilities.
Our staff are climbers themselves and are trained to show you the ropes in the safest and most enjoyable way possible. ICE has over
4 KM of climbing space between roped climbing, bouldering and auto belay.
So if you are a complete beginner or some one who has been climbing for years we’ve got something for you!!
